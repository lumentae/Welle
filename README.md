# Welle
An offline-first native and cross-platform Navidrome music player with low resource consumption. (the name comes from the German word 'welle' meaning 'wave')

## Motivation
I really love listening to music any chance I get, but I often don't have internet since I'm on drives a lot and I usually only have a bit of battery left, so I often disable my wifi and cellular data to save battery. Also, I have not yet found a good music player for android that works without any issues or one on a desktop platform that doesn't spawn a browser. That's why I'm making a fully offline and native music player!
